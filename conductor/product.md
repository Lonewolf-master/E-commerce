# Initial Concept

Aether Electronix is a "Spatial E-commerce" platform designed for high-end tech enthusiasts. It features:
- **Visual Language:** iOS Glassmorphism with layers of light, frosted glass panes, and haptic-feel animations using GSAP.
- **3D Product Experience:** High-fidelity 3D models (Three.js) that users can rotate, zoom, and "unbox" virtually.
- **AI Integration (Aether Concierge):** A natural language portal powered by Gemini for curated, intelligent product recommendations.
- **Seamless Interactions:** "Fly-to-Cart" effects, dynamic sorting with physical weight, and spatial context-aware features.
- **Real-time Collaboration:** A "Producer-Direct" chat with co-browsing and AI hand-off.
- **Admin Command Center:** An iOS-style minimalist dashboard for inventory, global discounts, and live traffic heatmaps.
- **Dynamic Environment:** Real-world time-based lighting and digital unboxing experiences.
- **System Integrity:** Decoupled architecture using Three.js/GSAP for experience, Gemini for intelligence, and WebSockets for real-time data.

# Product Definition: Aether Electronix

## Vision
Aether Electronix is a "Spatial E-commerce" platform designed for high-end tech enthusiasts. It aims to create a tactile, intelligent playground for tech enthusiasts that feels like an iOS app come to life.

## Target Users
- **High-end tech enthusiasts and early adopters:** Individuals who value high-fidelity experiences and cutting-edge technology.

## Core Features (MVP)
- **3D Product Stage (Three.js):** Users can rotate, zoom, and "unbox" gadgets in a high-fidelity 3D environment.
- **AI "Aether Concierge" (Gemini):** A natural language portal for curated, intelligent product recommendations.
- **iOS Glassmorphism (GSAP):** An interface built on "layers of light," frosted glass panes, and haptic-feel animations.

## Product Goals
- **High-end tech experience:** Provide a sophisticated, visually appealing, and tactile e-commerce environment.
- **Intelligent Shopping:** Use AI to curate and recommend products based on user intent and lifestyle.
- **Seamless Interaction:** Create a "Fluid OS" experience that goes beyond standard e-commerce grids.

## Conversion Strategy
- **Direct e-commerce purchase:** A seamless, standard checkout flow for high-velocity sales.

## Success Metrics
- **High user engagement time:** Measured by user interaction with 3D and AI features.
- **AI-driven conversion rate:** Specifically tracking sales through the "Aether Concierge."
- **ROAS for high-end tech segments:** Measuring the effectiveness of marketing spend on target demographics.

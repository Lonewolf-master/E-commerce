# Product Guidelines: Aether Electronix

## Voice and Communication
- **AI Concierge Tone:** Sophisticated, minimalist, and effortlessly helpful. Use "Active Precision" language.
- **Language Style:** Sophisticated but not cold. Use "accessible" instead of "cheap" and "high-velocity" instead of "fast."

## Visual Identity
- **Background Palette:** Deep Space Charcoal (#050505) with subtle, moving ambient gradients.
- **Surface Materials:** High-Refractive Glassmorphism with deep blur effects and razor-thin glowing borders.
- **Color Accents:** 
  - iOS Blue (#007AFF) for critical actions.
  - Stellar Silver (#F5F5F7) for text and high-end finishes.
  - Hyper-Pink (#FF2D55) for discounts and "Live" alerts.

## Interaction and Animation
- **Haptic-Feel Micro-interactions:** Every click has a "haptic" bounce. Buttons "squish" and expand using GSAP like physical materials.
- **Fluid Grid Physics:** Product sorting and filtering use GSAP Flip for seamless, physical-feeling rearrangements. Items feel like they have "visual weight."
- **3D Product Stages:** Smooth, high-fidelity rotation and zoom with hotspot technical specs revealed in glass bubbles.
- **Dynamic Environmental Lighting:** The entire platform reflects the user's real-world time (Solar Tracking) through GSAP color-tweens.

## Layout & Responsiveness
- **Universal Fluidity:** The experience MUST be fully responsive across all device sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop).
- **Mobile First Interaction:** On smaller screens, 3D interactions and glassmorphic layers should adapt to touch gestures, maintaining the "haptic" feel through high-performance animations.

## Branding
- **Logo:** A "living" 3D glyph that pulses with a soft blue neon glow during scroll.
- **Experience Philosophy:** Move away from "grid of boxes" to a "Fluid OS" experience.
